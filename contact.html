We plan our tours for individuals, for those wanting a larger group please contact us for more imformation and pricing.

We try our best to minimise cancellation costs if they do arise. There is however a considerable amount of admin involved in booking tours and itineraries, plus cancellation costs on our end so there will be a small fee if you decide to cancel your trip.

We recommend dressing for the occasion, informal and relaxed is the best way to go! For our warmer climates we recommend a simple t-shirt and shorts. Walking shoes are a must... Hiking shoes or shoes with a great grip are even better! For our colder climates we recommend a long sleeve top, pants, and a good thick jacket. If you are joinging us in the snow we do have rental snowsuits available to avoid too much baggage but if you would like to bring your own that is also welcome.
